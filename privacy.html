Privacy and safety

Sakha is currently a static GitHub Pages app. It does not require an account, public profile, or full student name.

Student identity

Use a nickname only. Sakha stores the nickname and last selected topic in this browser using local storage so the same device can continue smoothly.

Camera and media

The static app does not upload camera images. Camera/image-understanding claims should stay out of the product until true multimodal support and consent copy are implemented.

Voice

Voice input is optional and user initiated. Browser speech support may vary by device and browser.

AI requests

When online AI is used, the chat text needed for the answer is sent to the configured AI proxy. Do not type private personal details into the chat.

Tracking

The static app should not include ads, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics in the student flow.

Compliance status

This page is product guidance, not legal certification. Formal child-privacy or school compliance claims need legal review before public use.
