For parents

Sakha uses a nickname on this device and starts with questions, not direct answers. The goal is visible understanding: your child explains the idea back in their own words.

For teachers

Every concept is stored as a static concept pack. You can review topics, analogies, misconceptions, and practice questions without a dashboard or login.

Privacy and safety

No ads, no camera upload, and no public profile. Voice input uses browser speech support when available. Offline AI is optional and downloads only when selected.

What is offline mode?

Offline mode downloads a small AI model (200MB) to your device. After that, Sakha works completely without internet - no data usage, no connectivity needed.

When to use: Rural areas, limited data plans, or if you want privacy (nothing leaves your device).

Note: First download takes 2-5 minutes on WiFi.
